Key Observations

  • CauSumX achieves higher utility (92,404.77 vs 23,349.86)
  • Greedy provides significantly better coverage (94.53% vs 20.05%)
  • CauSumX focuses on a specific age group (18-24 years old)
  • Greedy selects more diverse conditions and treatments
  • Execution time is slightly lower for CauSumX (391.09s vs 481.67s)

Fairness Comparison

  • CauSumX: Higher utility but lower coverage, potentially less fair
  • Greedy: Lower utility but much higher and more balanced coverage
  • Protected coverage is higher for Greedy (94.85% vs 24.60%)
  • Greedy's rules cover a wider range of demographics

Conclusions

  • CauSumX: High utility, but narrow focus and low coverage
  • Greedy: Better coverage and diversity, more balanced approach
  • Trade-off between maximizing utility and ensuring fairness
  • Choice depends on specific use case priorities
  • Further research needed to optimize fairness-utility balance
